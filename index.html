How it works

The site polls public blockchain endpoints for the latest block height and average interval. The result drives a continuously updating sweep hand and countdown so you can feel the cadence of Bitcoin's heartbeat.

Network drift

When blocks are faster than the 10-minute target, difficulty ratchets up; when they lag, difficulty eases. The clock dramatizes that drift so traders, miners, and curious onlookers get instant intuition.
